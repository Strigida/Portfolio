About

I’m a UX Designer who’s gained Production Art skills along my journey! My passion for creating content that makes the world a more accessible and aesthetic place for all of us was born from this idea: form & function are two sides of the same coin. Every player, user, or client I interact with has a unique point of view, and understanding them allows me to create delightful games, simulations, and applications of great value.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a bachelor of science in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology (GIMM) from Boise State University, where also had the pleasure of working with the GIMM Development Team for several exceptional years, including clients in industry, education, & government. Since then, I’ve had the pleasure of working with other fascinating people and clients, on further content in this arena.

I love what I do, & look forward to where each turn in the path takes me!

On my off time I’m an audio engineer & vocalist for the band Evil-Liza. I also work with a small band of loyal friends who create games & movies. I enjoy hiking, tea parties (yes, the kind with porcelain tea cups & tiny cookies), travel, photography, & reading excellent books in warm windows.
