ARt Visualizer

TRY ART ON YOUR WALLS BEFORE YOU BUY IT

The ARt Visualizer app is an in progress project I’ve created to allow users to display 2D art in a 3D frame (user chooses a style from a selection) in order to see it in your space before you buy it. This way users can decide between multiple choices of art and framing in order to better suit their tastes, furniture, or even see if it works in a friend’s house (surprise gifts that won’t clash!). It uses Swift in xCode with ARKit to create a mobile app for apple devices.
